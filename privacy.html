Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Viral Space Defender ("we", "our", "the Game") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Web3 gaming application.

2. Information We Collect

Wallet Address: When you connect your TON wallet, we receive your public wallet address. We do not have access to your private keys.

Telegram Data: If you access the Game through Telegram, we may receive your Telegram user ID, username, and profile information as provided by the Telegram Mini App SDK.

Game Data: We collect gameplay data such as scores, levels, and in-game purchases to provide and improve the Game experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: (a) provide and operate the Game; (b) process in-game transactions; (c) improve and personalize your experience; (d) communicate with you about updates and features.

4. Blockchain Transactions

All TON transactions are recorded on the public blockchain. Transaction data, including wallet addresses and amounts, is publicly visible and immutable.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with: (a) service providers who assist in operating the Game; (b) legal authorities when required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide the Game services and comply with legal obligations.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the Game or our official channels.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through our official Telegram channel.
